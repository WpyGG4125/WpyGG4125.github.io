Variable Guide

Use these in your templates to automatically pull data from the generator inputs:

  • {{name}}: The recipient's name.
  • {{event}}: The title of the event/meeting.
  • {{date}}: The date of the event.
  • {{time}}: The start time.
  • {{loc}}: The physical or virtual location.
  • {{link}}: The URL for registration or more info.
  • {{salutation}}: Automatically calculates "Cher/Chère" or "Mr/Ms" based on gender, or uses your custom input.

Note: Placeholders are case-sensitive. Use exactly as shown.
